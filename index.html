Who am I?

My name is Luca Santomassimo and i'm 39 years old. My education starts with an industrial technical institute where I chose to graduate as an electrical engineer, and almost immediately I started working in some factories as a warehouse worker.

In 2019, after winning a public competition, I entered a veterinary research institute as a Data Entry, where I stay until 2024.

During this period, given the pandemic that hit us, I was in charge of managing health operations (molecular swabs, vaccines, blood draws, etc.) in close contact with local ASL, and thanks to these experiences I was able to mature both professional and personal.

Given my passion for web development and digital graphics, I created my own brand that I called “Orpheus Design”.

During this time, I also earned a bachelor's degree in business economics in 2022
Here I finally decide to try to change my life and enter the world of tech, my true passion, by enrolling in the Epicode degree program.
